What is Bunny Island?


Bunny Island is an on-chain game that allows users to breed, modify, and trade virtual Bunnies.

Every aspect of the game is supervised by a Plutus smart-contract, from breeding subsequent generations to modifying the Bunnies genome through special items and even shopping at a in-game store.

Essentially this will be a machine that is always fair; can't be corrupted, can not rug, doesn't get tired, and makes no compromises, the ultimate ruler over Bunny Island.

A New Kind of NFT


The current trend in Cardano is the CIP-0025 NFT Metadata standard.

This kind of tokens store the NFT data on the metadata of the transaction that minted the token. As such, it relies on the fact that the minting operation is executed by a central authority that will verify that the metadata is correct; however, in a completely decentralized system, this is undesirable. We want decentralization, after all.

Bunny Island NFTs solve the centralization issues with CIP-0025, we will share more datails soon. Stay tuned.





Frequently Ask Question


What is an NFT?

A non-fungible token (NFT) is a unit of data stored on the blockchain that certifies digital assets to be unique. These assets can then be tracked to provide proof of ownership.

What blockchain is it on?

Bunny Island tokens will be a new kind of NFTs droping on Cardano.

How is Bunny Island different from other NFT projects?

Bunny Island aims to be the first 100% decentralized NFT project on the Cardano blockchain, governed by a smart contract. Every aspect of the game will be run by Plutus code, from breeding subsequent generations to modifying the Bunnies gnome through special items and even the in-game store. Essentially this will be a machine that is always fair; can't be corrupted, can not rug, doesn't get tired, and makes no compromises, the ultimate ruler over Bunny Island.

After deploying it, the game will exist as long as the Cardano blockchain exists (essentially forever).

We are taking several measures to ensure the game stays relevant for many decades and hopefully outlives us all. Stay tuned for more details.

How can I get a Bunny?

You will be able to mint one directly from out smart-contract (Yes, thats right, smart-contract minting on Cardano!) during the gen-0 mint event, And If you already own some bunnies you will be able to mint your own!.

How much will minting Bunnies cost?

The mint cost of gen-0 bunnies is still TBD. The breeding fee for creating new bunnies from your own will be 4 ADA.

Are Bunny Island NFTs native tokens?

Yes, we believe that having NFTs as native tokens is one of the strengths of Cardano. We are just changing the place where the metadata is stored to allow this data to be read by smart-contracts and, more importantly, to allow smart-contracts to validate pre and post conditions on all new NFTs minted through the breeding mechanism of the game.

We will share more details soon.

How many traits will the Bunnies have?

Each Bunnies genome consist of 10 gens, each gene can take 50 different values. (this adds up to 97 quadrillions)

When is the drop?

We are aiming for Q4 2022. However, this can move closer or farther to today; this will depend on how fast we can advance on the development.

How many Gen-0 Bunnies will be minted?

We are still discussing how many, but we will share the number as soon as we reach a desition.

What wallets can I use to mint and play the game?

You will need to use a dApp enabled wallet. Right now we are planing to support Nami, Eternl and Flint, but we may add more in the near future.
